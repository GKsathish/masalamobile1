New Reliance Jio prepaid recharge prices for 2022 revealed; Check out the BEST plans you should go for

The new Reliance Jio prepaid recharge prices have been revealed. The telecom company announced its new prepaid recharge prices back on December 1, 2021. The revised recharge prices witnessed a nearly 20% hike for all plans. So, it is important to know how much you will have to pay now to get the most popular Jio prepaid recharge plans in 2022. We have curated a list of Jio prepaid recharge plans you should be considering.

Reliance Jio new prepaid recharge prices for 2022

Reliance Jio offers plans starting from 14 days to all the way up to 365 days. After the revision, all of the Jio plans have seen a price hike. The hike for Jio came after Airtel and Vodafone Idea both increased their prepaid recharge plans. For all Jio users, it has now become important to know which plan will serve them the best. We have curated the list based on internet usage so pick the ones depending on how heavily you use Jio and based on that make the ideal pick.

Check out the best Reliance Jio prepaid recharge prices and plans

1. Light internet users: As far as the best Reliance Jio prepaid plans are concerned, this one is for the light internet users- someone who uses mobile data for WhatsApp messages, checking social media occasionally and Google searches or other low data-intensive usages. This Reliance Jio plan is also good for someone who has access to WiFi at home or at work and doesn’t use mobile data a lot. You should opt for Reliance Jio 1GB per day prepaid recharge plan. Jio prepaid recharge price for this plan is Rs. 209 for a validity of 28 days. Out of all the other plans, this is the most cost-effective.

2. Moderate internet users: This group will have users checking social media more frequently, downloading images and videos on WhatsApp, and watching some videos on YouTube. If this description fits you, you should go for the Reliance Jio recharge plan of 1.5GB per day. It is also their most popular tier. You can get 28 days validity at a recharge price of Rs. 239. However, if you can afford it, you should opt for Rs. 666 plan which will give you a validity of 84 days. This is the most cost-effective plan in this tier.

3. Heavy internet user: This category will include users who watch Instagram Reels, Facebook videos, YouTube content, and occasional Netflix and HotStar movies or shows. For you, Reliance Jio prepaid recharge plan of 2GB per day will suffice. The 28 days plan costs Rs. 299. However, if you can afford it, you should go for the 84 days plan which comes at Rs. 719. Do note, there is another recharge plan at Rs. 1066 for 84 days which also gives you a free subscription to Disney+ Hotstar for the period of the recharge. If you want to watch Disney+ Hotstar content, you can also opt for the other plan.
